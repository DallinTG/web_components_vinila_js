Marcin Matuszny in Canada_part 1


PART 1 Coming for a month-long trip to Canada and you can only take one bike, what would it be? Marcin Matuszny aka. Banan chose Hornet Pro! Well, most of you who don't know him may be surprised, but this hardtail warrior was truly happy with his choice! Marcin literally conquered the whole Whistler Bikepark - a feat that is impressive in itself. He spiced it with a trip to the north shore mecca of North Vancouver, which has a few trails we can't name due to the digger's hard labor, Squamish, and Pemberton's finest. Enough? Nah, Marcin went for Coast Gravity Park! Bears, massive features, lovely views, and a feeling there is much more to shred! Riding daily requires great skills and strength, which many may underestimate. We are in awe Marcin does it with no rear suspension, but damn, these pictures don't lie so enjoy and Ride Your Way!
